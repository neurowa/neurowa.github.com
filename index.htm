The Synapse Laboratory

Who we are:

Synapse is a collaborative research group primarily exploring areas of cognitive neuroscience. Our team is one of the leading Parkinson's research groups in Australia, and is conducting a world-first study of neuronal excitability in chronic lower back pain.

Our team is based at Curtin University, and has affiliations with the Cambridge Parkinson’s Disease Research Clinic, the University of Western Australia, and the Parkinson’s Association of Western Australia.
